How it works

Our contactless delivery system has a defined process flow that completely prevents direct contact between your patients, our lab ninjas and your laboratory team. It is as follows:

  • Call center agent receives your request.
  • Call center agent contacts your patient on the scheduled delivery day.
  • Your request is assigned to a LabBank Ninja
  • LabNinja rides to your patient's doorstep.
  • Lab Ninja calls the patient and explains terms of the delivery scheme - No contact, No exchange of items
  • Once patients step out, Lab Ninja opens the section button by pressing a button.
  • Patients put his/her sample in this temperature regulated compartment.
  • LabNinja repeats the same step to open the waste section.
  • Patient puts any medical waste into the Biohazard bag.
  • Lab Ninja updates pickup status on the app - you get a notification!
  • Lab Ninja rides to drop off samples and waste in your laboratory - Same terms of pickup applies.
  • Lab Ninja completes the order on his app and you are asked to confirm and rate the delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

Why Self Collection?

Self collection protects our healthcare workers from exposure to potential infection. It is also more convenient and efficient rather than driving to a center. It also makes it easy for certain groups like the sick and elderly to get tested.

 Is Self Collection acceptable

Yes, our kit comes with detailed instructions and an audio guide to ensure patients collect samples safely and effectively. If you follow all instructions carefully you will get samples that yield accurate results, just like a sample collected by a professional.

 How do I order a kit?

As a lab partner you can easily request for as many kits as you want from your dashboard. Individuals have to request for our kits with our registered lab partners.

 Why is the Contactless Delivery style better?

Our contactless pick up and drop off delivery model ensures that there is zero contact between the patient, our rider and your laboratory staff. It promotes social distancing and removes all forms of person-person infection transmission.

When do I get my results?

Kindly contact the diagnostic center where you ordered the kit from to get information about the test turnaround time.
