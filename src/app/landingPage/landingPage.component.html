Problem

Unfettered access to rapid and safe COVID-19 tests is one of the most critical factors in reducing the spread of the disease. There is a need for a simple collection method that promotes social distancing, reduces the load and pressure on the diagnostic centers and vastly increases the daily testing capacity of the diagnostic centers.

Solution

LifeBank has developed a simple personal sample collection kit that provides a convenient and reliable option for patient sample collection from the comfort and safety of their home. It is a simple all-inclusive kit that contains all the necessary equipment to collect both nasal and oral swab samples viable for the COVID-19 RT-PCR Screening Test.

Each box includes easy to understand instructions to guide you through sample collection, sample labelling, waste disposal and how to properly package your sample to return to the laboratory personnel for processing.

Kit Components

  • One viral transmission medium
  • One Nasal Swab stick
  • One Oral Swab sticks
  • Two pairs of medical hand gloves
  • One Tongue Depressor
  • One Biohazard waste disposal bag
  • One simple to use Instruction guide.

Benefits

  • Patients sampling and transport is safe and reliable
  • Your staff are protected from exposure to potential infection.
  • Ease of access to testing facilities for some groups, such as the elderly and the sick.
  • Improved lead time with optimum cost-effectiveness.
  • Restrict movement and reduce the person-person transfer of the virus by limiting contact.

Get your kit!

You can either opt to pick up the kits yourself or have us deliver them to you using our contactless delivery system which completely removes any form of contact between your patients, our lab ninjas and your laboratory team.

Our delivery process flow is as follows:

  1. Call center agents receives your request and assigns to a Lab Ninja
  2. Lab Ninja picks up your order and contacts you on the scheduled delivery day
  3. LabNinja rides to your doorstep.
  4. Lab Ninja calls you and further explains the terms of the delivery scheme - No contact, No exchange of items, Follow directions on the body of the vehicle.
  5. Once you step out, Lab Ninja opens the section where your kit(s) is by pressing a button
  6. You collect the kit from the kit section.
  7. Lab Ninja updates the delivery status and you get a notification!
  8. You confirm and rate delivery!
